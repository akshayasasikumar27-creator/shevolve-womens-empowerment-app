# SHEvolve – Women’s Health & Empowerment Platform

SHEvolve is a digital platform designed to empower women through health support,
career growth, safety resources, financial tools, and community connection.

This project was created for a hackathon focused on women's empowerment and well-being.

Figma Prototype
https://www.figma.com/design/qncjbEm8hAlBnrVNmP0Z1V/Shevolve

Features
- Women’s Health Hub
- Cycle & wellness tracking
- Career and mentorship support
- Financial empowerment tools
- Community support
- Gender-based violence & safety resources

Goal
To provide a safe, inclusive, and accessible digital ecosystem supporting women
across different stages of life.
